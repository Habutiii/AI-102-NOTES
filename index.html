Exam Overview

What is AI-102?

The Azure AI Engineer Associate certification validates your ability to design, build, and deploy AI solutions on Azure using Azure AI Services, Azure OpenAI, and related tools. Candidates typically have C#, Python, or JavaScript experience and familiarity with Azure fundamentals.

Exam Skill Domains

Exam Tip NLP is the largest domain (30–35%). Focus on Azure AI Language, CLU, Translator, Speech, and Q&A. Generative AI has grown to 15–20% — cover Azure OpenAI, RAG, and Azure AI Foundry thoroughly.
